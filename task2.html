absolute position relative position
  • Absolute positioning removes an element from the document flow and positions it relative to its nearest positioned ancestor.
  • Absolute positioning can overlap or disrupt other elements.
  • Absolute location helps to determine the location of a place with respect to certain coordinates that themselves have a fixed reference.
  • Relative positioning maintains the element's position within the document flow.
  • The relative position of a place is determined in reference to certain landmarks or known locations.
